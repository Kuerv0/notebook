What's a crucial conversation?
  - Opinions vary
  - Stakes are high
  - Emotions run strong

The outcome could have a huge impact on either relationships or results that
affect you greatly.

You can measure the health of relationships, teams, and organizations by
measuring the lag time between when problems are identified and when they are
resolved.

We tend to avoid crucial conversations and handle them poorly.

When it matters most, we tend to do our worst.
